Africa abounds with incredible sights, from soaring sand dunes to savannas teeming with wildlife. Experience the continent’s many wonders on trips to Tanzania, South Africa, Botswana, Rwanda, Morocco, and more. Witness the annual wildebeest migration on safari in the Serengeti, or explore the sun-washed cities of Morocco through your camera lens. In Tanzania, spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more on a thrilling family safari.
